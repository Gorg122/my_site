Schloß Neuschwanstein [nɔy'ʃvanʃtain], literally "New Swan stone (rock)") — romantic castle of the Bavarian king Ludwig II near the town of Fussen and Hohenschwangau castle in southwest Bavaria, near the Austrian border.
